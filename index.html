Background

Operation KARE was founded at the Zeta Phi chapter of the Kappa Alpha Order at High Point University back in 2016 by a group of students with an idea on making the world a better place by helping every wounded veteran that they could. Now, Operation KARE has branched out to other Kappa Alpha chapters and more veterans than ever before are able to have an improved lifestyle thanks to everyone involved.

On April 23, 2016, the members of the Kappa Alpha Order at High Point University surprised a local veteran with a Track Chair purchased from the Independence Fund after fundraising over $26,000 to purchase the chair and give the remainder to the Independence Fund, helping out many other veterans in the process.
